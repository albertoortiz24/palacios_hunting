What to expect

Hunters should fly from US through Phoenix, AZ and then ONLY to Hermosillo, Sonora, Mexico, NOT Mexico City. Once you arrive in Hermosillo, hunters will walk to the gate and go through customs. Hunters with gun permit will need to present it. If Palacios Hunting Outfitter has your permit, there will be a military soldier that will check your gun in at that time. Our crew will be there to meet you outside the glass door and take you to the ranch.

To assist with any airline problems, please have your gun permit application WITH you.

Weapons

It is recommended that you bring your own weapon. Weapons are inspected upon entry at the airport and you will be issued a temporary local license. Please ensure that you receive a valid customs declaration form from customs in your own country to ensure re-importation of weapons on your return. Have all your serial numbers at hand to avoid problems at customs.

You are permitted one weapon and 200 rounds of ammo per gun. Any additional guns the license permit charge is $300 per gun.
