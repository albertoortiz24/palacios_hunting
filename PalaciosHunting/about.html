About Us

Palacios Hunting Outfitters is headquartered in Hermosillo, Sonora, Mexico. We provide the highest quality hunting experience in the region, since we have the expertise that comes from having born and hunted in Sonora. You will always be hunting free range ranches. We believe in hunting trophy deer in their natural habitat. We never over hunt our ranches and only harvest mature animals to ensure healthy herds for the future.

We offer first class trophy Mule deer, Coues deer, Desert Bighorn sheep, Gould turkey and as an added feature a Deep sea fishing package in the sea of Cortez. Our unique mix of desert flats and rocky hills gives us a great view of the country and an exhilarating memorable hunting experience. We are a family run operation consisting of uncles, cousins and family friends, our crew will greet you with a smile and make you feel right at home. Together, we will make sure you will have many memorable stories to share when you get back home. We invite you, your family and friends to come and share this adventure with us and we will make sure you will leave with a big smile. We sincerely hope you will allow us to help you with your hunt of a life time. Please feel free to contact Charles Francis (320) 223-1279 for any questions.
